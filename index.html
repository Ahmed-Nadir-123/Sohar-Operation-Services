About Sohar Operation Services

Sohar Operation Services Company L.L.C. (SOS) is the dedicated Operations and Maintenance contractor for Myah Gulf Oman Desalination Company (MGODC), operating the Sohar 4 Independent Water Project—one of the largest seawater reverse osmosis desalination plants in the Sultanate of Oman, with a production capacity of 250,000 cubic meters per day.

Our Journey

From establishment to excellence, discover the milestones that shaped our commitment to water solutions

2019

SOS Established

Sohar Operation Services LLC was established as the dedicated Operations and Maintenance contractor for Myah Gulf Oman Desalination Company (MGODC), marking the beginning of our commitment to operational excellence in water solutions.

August 2019

Commercial Operations Begin

The Sohar 4 Independent Water Project commenced commercial operations with a massive capacity of 250,000 m³ of desalinated water per day, becoming Oman's second-largest desalination facility and serving over 220,000 people.

2020

First ISO Certifications

SOS achieved multiple ISO certifications including ISO 9001 (Quality Management), ISO 14001 (Environmental Management), and ISO 45001 (Occupational Health & Safety), establishing our commitment to international standards.

2021

SOS Laboratory® Accreditation

Our in-house laboratory achieved ISO/IEC 17025 accreditation, enabling comprehensive water quality testing, microbiological analysis, and heavy metal detection services with internationally recognized standards.

2022

Asset Management Excellence

SOS achieved ISO 55001 certification for Asset Management and ISO 50001 for Energy Management, demonstrating our commitment to efficient resource utilization and sustainable operations.

2023

LTI-Free Operations

Achieved a significant safety milestone with Lost Time Injury (LTI) free operations, reinforcing our ISO 45001 certification and demonstrating unwavering commitment to workplace safety and employee wellbeing.

2024

Academic Partnerships & Recognition

Established strategic partnerships with five academic institutions through formal MOUs: University of Technology and Applied Sciences (Sohar & Shinas), Sohar University, IMCO, and Al Kaboura Vocational College for training and research initiatives supporting Oman Vision 2040.

2025 & Beyond

Sustainable Future

Continuing our journey toward innovation and sustainability with advanced carbon capture technology, expanded research partnerships, and pioneering solutions that will shape the future of water management in the region.
