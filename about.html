Who are we

Sohar Operation Services Company (SOS) is the operations and maintenance contractor for the Sohar 4 Independent Water Project (IWP).

This project is one of Oman's largest seawater reverse osmosis desalination plants.

What we have

Advanced SWRO Technology

Largest seawater reverse osmosis desalination plant in Oman with 1.3km intake system

Operational Excellence

Preventive and corrective maintenance with strict regulatory compliance

Consortium Partnership

Owned by MGODC consortium: Oman Brunei Investment Company & Sogex Oman Co.

Quality Assurance

ISO/IEC 17025 accredited laboratory and water quality management
